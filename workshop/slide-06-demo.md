Let's See It

## Naveen drives a tuk-tuk.

He has one price: **$0.40 per kilometer.**

You see it. You pay it. He drives.

---

### What we'll see:

**Naveen's digital payment page:**
- Shows his price upfront ($0.40/km)
- You scan or click to pay
- Payment happens in USDC (stable, not speculative)
- Transaction recorded on the ledger
- No negotiation, no surprises

### Why this works:

**For Naveen:**
- No haggling
- Gets paid instantly
- Price is fair and visible
- Builds trust with customers

**For customers:**
- Know the price before asking
- Pay quickly and easily
- No surprises
- Can verify the payment


**Let's see it in action.**
