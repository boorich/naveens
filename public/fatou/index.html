Getting started with me

01

Discovery Call

We'll hop on a 20–30 minute intro call to discuss your goals, challenges, and what kind of support you need. This is where I listen, ask the right questions, and help you map out what's possible.

02

Custom Proposal

After the call, I'll send over a customized proposal outlining services, timelines, tools, and pricing — all aligned to your business priorities.

03

Onboarding & Setup

Once the agreement is signed, you invite me to your current workspace or tools (Notion, Slack, Google Drive, etc.). Don't have one? no panic, I will set it up. We will clarify workflows, and establish our preferred communication rhythm.

04

Execution & Collaboration

I start executing deliverables, managing your calendar, organizing your vendors, handling research — whatever's on your plate, I've got it covered.
